Lot of benefits of Gardening. Some of them are:

Fresh Environment

Home Gardening reduces pollution in air, gives out cool breezes and pleasant aroma.

Relieves Stress

The amount of stress is reduced to a great level when working in the garden and improves mental health.

Lowers Blood Pressure

Because of the physical- activity involved in home gardening ,it can lower the blood pressure and improve immune system.

Eat Healthy

Healthy source of freshly grown veggies which is harmful-chemical free.

Hobby

It is a pleasant hobby,after spending time in gardening,it allows you to focus on other activity that benefits you.

Be Happy

Serotonin which is a happy chemical released in the brain when spending time in gardening,Serotonin is a natural anti-depressant.

Here are the steps for a Beautiful Home Gardening

1. Getting Started
Collection of neccessary tools required such as soil scoop, watering can, gloves and sharp scissors.
2. Soil Preparation
Your soil might contain chemicals so soil-chemical testing is neccessary, if you dont have access to it dont worry,try visiting pot-planter's garden and get the soil by yourself for your favourite pots.It might be tedious work but i promise, you'll get plenty of aftermath reward from your efforts.
3. Good compost making
The compost helps in keeping the plant nourished and moistured which is very essential.You can follow the conventional way of maintaining a terracota pot piling it up with dry leaves,kitchen scrapes etc.,it takes two months in hot weather and little longer in winter for its conversion.Add a thin layer of soil or saw dust to avoid bad smell(usaully it won't smell bad).Keep it least moistured.
4. Choosing the Right Plants
Choose the plant according to the soiltype, light, moisture and season. Buying plants from pot-planter's could be a second option as many Plants are best grown from seed by our own hands.
5. Planting the Crops
This is the important phase whatever you are planting let it be herbs, veggies,flowers or decorative or indoor plants.
6.Watering
Watering the plants in the morning is suggested to avoid soil being too damp entire night.Infrequent watering must be totally avoided for good growth outcome.
7.Protecting Plants Without Pesticides
If there's attack of the predators make sure your plant is getting enough sunlight, water and nutrients.Some insects such as lady bug won't affect your plants.
8. Harvesting
Finally the fruits of your labour must be harvested.It produces more as you harvest more.If it's leafy greens harvest it along with their seed, harvest herbs in the morning for its freshness.Don't pluck it with your fingers as you'll be damaging the plant-tissuse instead use knife or scissor.
9. maintainance
If there's sick plants or dried plant remove it, to encourage the plant growth and use it for compost.
